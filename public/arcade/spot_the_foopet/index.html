Arcade » Spot the Foopet

Instructions

  1. Watch as the pets are shown one at a time.
  2. When all of the pets are shown, pick the missing one of out a line-up.
  3. Win 10 FooGems for each level passed.
  4. Each level will get harder.
  5. Your FooGems are only awarded at the end of the game, so keep going until the game is over.
  6. The person with the high score each day will receive a trophy as a gift that can be used in decorating your scenes.
  7. You can earn up to 10,000 FG per day when combined with other activities.
