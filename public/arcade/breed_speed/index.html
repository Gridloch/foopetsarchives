Instructions

  • Click on the rotating images where the name matches the breed - as quickly as you can.
    • Wrong matches will subtract from your total score (and FooGems) so be careful!

    • Level 1 starts with one (1) pet. Level 8 will have eight (8) pets.
    • There are eight (8) levels total.
  • Your FooGems score will display after you complete level 8. Try to beat your previous score!
