Arcade » Foozle Tiles

Instructions

  • You have about one second to study a picture before the tile order is scrambled.
  • Swap tiles to recover the original picture in the least amount of moves possible - as quickly as you can. (You will be timed.)
  • You will have three (3) puzzle pictures to unscramble.
  • FooGems will be awarded based on the total number of moves and time. Remember, less is more!
  • Your stats will display after you complete the third and last picture. Try to beat your previous score!
