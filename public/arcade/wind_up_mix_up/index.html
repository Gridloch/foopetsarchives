Arcade » Wind Up Mix Up

Instructions

  • Before your time runs out, move the wall up and down until all the red mice are on the red side, and the blue mice are on the blue side.
    • The mice will need to pass through the hole in the wall.
    • Use the walls to block the mice from going to the wrong side.
  • First level starts with two mice for each side and 40 seconds to complete the task.
  • Each new level will introduce one more mice for each side and five (5) additional seconds to complete the task.
  • The mice will move faster after each level, so get ready!
