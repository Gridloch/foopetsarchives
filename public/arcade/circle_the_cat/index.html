Arcade » Circle the Cat

Instructions

  1. Click on the hexagonal shapes to create pillars around the cat.
  2. Try to isolate the cat in as few steps as possible!
  3. Win FooGems based on the number of steps you were able to isolate the cat in.
  4. The person with the high score each day will receive a trophy as a gift that can be used in decorating your scenes.
  5. You can earn up to 10,000 FG per day when combined with other activities.
