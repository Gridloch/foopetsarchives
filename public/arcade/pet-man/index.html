Arcade » Pet Man

Instructions

  • Pick up as many balls of yarn or tennis balls as you can, while avoiding the Animal Control Officer.
  • When you pick up a bone, you can chase the Animal Control Officer back into his cage!
  • Each level gets more difficult, so stay on your toes!
  • Be sure and finish your game to earn FooGems and record your score.
  • You can earn up to 10,000 FG per day when combined with other activities.
